Forward

The past few years have felt like a roller coaster. I felt the rush after reaching my highest point, descending downward until I crash and burn. I’ve struggled to get back up on my feet so many times, but I always ended up in the same place. In that place was where I threw away the mindset that drove me here. But it’s the same place where I found the courage to forgive myself. I felt that this was what I needed. For the first time in a long time, I have a fresh start. No expectations; with only one question in my mind, “Where do you want to go?”

It’s easy to look at what others have and believe you want the same things. I’ve felt this way for a long time - thinking “If I did this, then I’ll be happy” or “If I got that job, then I’ll be successful as them.” But at some point, I realized that I don’t want to be living someone else’s life or chasing someone else’s dream - I want to be living in mine.

I did a lot of self-reflection, wondering what I really wanted to do. I spent days researching my options, and I decided to pursue my Master’s degree to specialize in Artificial Intelligence. There were many sleepless nights, wondering if I’m making the wrong decision. But I found clarity within myself after re-reading my intentions for this year and explaining it to my closes friends of this decision.

My journey ahead is as scary and uncertain as ever - but I want to take this leap of faith and seize this opportunity - knowing that this is what I want for myself. When I feel like giving up, I want to come back to this post and remind myself why.

It’s a little late, but happy twenty-twenty one! I’m looking forward to what comes my way :)
